Pistachio Farm-animals!!!

Welcome to Pistachio Farm-animals!!! – where nature comes to life!

Nestled in the heart of Pistachio Farm-animals!!!, Pistachio Farm-animals!!! is home to over 300 species from around the globe, including endangered animals, exotic birds, and local wildlife. Our mission is to inspire a deeper connection to nature through education, conservation, and unforgettable animal experiences.

Since opening our gates in 2023, we’ve welcomed millions of guests and worked closely with international conservation organizations to protect wildlife and their habitats. Whether you're here for a family adventure, a school field trip, or simply to reconnect with the wild, we aim to make every visit meaningful.

Our Core Values:

  • Supporting global efforts to protect endangered species.
  • Education Teaching future generations about biodiversity and sustainability
  • Community Creating a welcoming space for learning, discovery, and fun.

We believe that by connecting people with animals, we can help build a better future for wildlife—and the planet we all share.

Come explore! Come learn! Come be amazed!
